Product Design

What is Circles

Circles is a social network that allows users to replicate their real-life social circles focussing on design and usability.

What I’m doing for Circles

I am the creator and designer of Circles. Currently in talks with developers to release the app in the App Store.

Not re-inventing the wheel

Circles, being built on the basis of user’s frustrations of cluttered, unfocused, privacy intrusive (and sometimes with toxic traits) existing social media platforms tries to eliminate all of these while giving the experience of what social media was and can be a refreshing feel.

Thinking outside the “box”

By giving users the ability to create custom “circles” as most do in real-life they get released from the pressure of sharing things with any (group of) people they might not feel comfortable with sharing. Cutting down on clutter and emphasizing social circles with natural forms of media will increase engagement and a personal connection with the app

Social Media lacks a spark

User frustrations points show that my initial informal observations were mostly accurate and what potential additional features are.

Hyper-focus, circles.

Social circles were clear to become the main focus of Circles, a distinctive interface would be a requirement to separate it from the existing platforms. A bubbly interface with a lot of circles and rounded corners will make it obvious that a user is in the Circles app and communicate its philosophy at all times.
