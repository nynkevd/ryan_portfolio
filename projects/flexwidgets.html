Product Design

What is Flex Widgets

An app to let users create fully custom widgets with an array of design tools and information to display, right at their finger tips.

What I did in this project

My role in Flex Widgets was leading the product design of the app.

Yannik Schrade: developer and creator of Flex Widgets
Nikias Molina: Marketing and initial video material for Flex Widgets

The user was forgotten

Flex Widgets was unintuitive; items to add were unclear, the app lacked visual hierarchy and segmenting, the look and experience of the app were unattractive and impractical.

Satisfying expectations

By implementing all established default features users expect from an iOS app and categorizing the app’s functions into their respective segments Flex Widgets will enable users to utilize every part of Flex Widgets making it more inviting and fun to use.

It didn’t feel like an app

User research proved my initial reaction of the app right, it showed the empty space on the left drew too much attention or threw users off, the top selection falsely indicated a process to be followed and the app’s abilities were not represented.

A deep dive

Early on we decided Flex Widgets had the opportunity to stand out by polling the interface and making it stand out. I tried implementing all the new features and structures while exploring a neumorphic, dark, light and colored looks.

What goes where

Flex Widgets needed a place to build the widgets and a place to finalize them. These became the editing and exporting personas. Editing has all the tools on the left, items below the widget/canvas and layers on the right. The exporting persona allows users to give the widget a name, save the theme of the widget so the next ones can look identical in style or share it online.
